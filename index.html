01 — About

A dancer whothinks deeply.

01

I am majoring in Korean Dance at Sungkyunkwan University, where I explore the profound art of expressing human emotion through the movement of the body. For me, dance is not merely a performance — it is a conversation between the performer and the audience, a dialogue written without words.

My academic curiosity extends beyond the stage. I am deeply interested in analyzing how audiences experience performances — what creates satisfaction, what evokes connection, and how artistic expression leaves lasting impressions. I believe understanding the audience is as essential as mastering the craft itself.

I embrace learning as a continuous journey, always seeking to add new skills and refine existing ones — step by deliberate step.
